How to Play:

  1. Controlling the Snake

    Use the arrow keys or WASD to move the snake:

    • Up (W / ↑)to move the snake upward

    • Down (S / ↓) to move the snake downward

    • Left(A / ←) to move the snake left

    • Right(D / →) to move the snake right

  2. Eating the Apples

    • The snake will grow each time it eats an apple that appears randomly on the screen.

    • The more apples the snake eats, the longer it gets, making it harder to navigate.

  3. Avoiding Collisions

    • Don't let the snake collide with the walls or its own body. If it does, the game is over.

    • Try to navigate carefully as the snake becomes longer with each piece of food consumed.

  4. How Scoring Works

    • Each apple you eat increases your score

    • The longer the snake, the higher your score
